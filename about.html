About CardMania

CardMania is a comprehensive trading card price database and analysis platform designed to help collectors, traders, and enthusiasts make informed decisions about their card investments.

Our Mission

We aggregate and analyze real-time sales data from major marketplaces to provide accurate, up-to-date pricing information for trading cards across all major sports and collectible card games.

What We Do

  • Track sold listings from eBay and other marketplaces
  • Analyze price trends and market movements
  • Provide detailed card information and specifications
  • Help collectors manage and value their collections
  • Offer market insights and investment analytics

Data Sources

Our price data comes from actual completed sales on major platforms including:

  • eBay sold listings
  • PWCC Marketplace
  • Heritage Auctions
  • And more coming soon...

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. When you authorize CardMania to access your eBay account, we only request the minimum permissions necessary to provide our services. We never store your eBay password and you can revoke access at any time.
